# Reading Responses Folder

## Instructions

Save all your reading responses in this folder with the naming convention:
- `week01-response.pdf` (Week 1 only)
- `week02-response.md`
- `week03-response.md`
- ... through `week09-response.md`

## Format Requirements

### Week 1
- **Format**: PDF
- **Length**: 2-3 pages (600-900 words)
- **Due**: 9:00 AM, Monday, January 26

### Weeks 2-9
- **Format**: Markdown (.md)
- **Length**: 2-3 pages (600-900 words)
- **Due**: 9:00 AM on the day of class when readings are assigned

## Content Guidelines

Your responses should:
- Engage substantively with the assigned readings
- Make connections across multiple readings
- Relate concepts to your research interests
- Pose critical questions
- Show original thinking

**CRITICAL**: Reading responses must be written entirely by you. **No AI assistance permitted.**

## Grading Criteria

Each response worth 1% of final grade (9 total = 9%):
- Demonstrates careful reading (40%)
- Critical engagement with ideas (30%)
- Clear writing and organization (20%)
- Originality of thought (10%)

## Tips

- Don't just summarize - analyze and critique
- Connect to other readings and class discussions
- Use specific examples from the texts
- Write in your own voice
- Proofread before submitting
